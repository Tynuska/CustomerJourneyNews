HUX SPACE coming soon

Plug & Charge available with Powerpass plans
Plug & Charge (PnC) is a new public charging authentication method. It is available for ENYAQ iV models with ME 3.1 software. With PnC, the charging process can be started easily at compatible charging points by simply plugging in the charging cable. Customers no longer need a smartphone app or an RFID card, as the car is automatically identified at the charging point. PnC functionality is already supported in the IONITY network, with more partners set to follow. Existing Powerpass users can activate the feature with a few clicks via the MyŠKODA app, while new customers will get PnC when they sign a contract. PnC is supported by the Powerpass Simple Charge and Charge Faster plans.

Flow Design System Guidelines
The ŠKODA UX Team (VX/1) has released Flow guidelines – new CI documentation tailored to digital touchpoints. What is Flow? A fully comprehensive Design System used to create and scale a wide range of digital products and experiences. This multi-platform Design System integrates guidelines, tools and resources to help designers and developers create digital products and deliver a cohesive experience across all our digital touchpoints. With Flow, you can create digital products faster than ever before. It considerably reduces the time needed for coding, component implementation and redesign work. You can access the guidelines at http://flow.skoda-brand.com/.

Seven new Human Touch Masters appointed
At the start of September, WDC Prague hosted an impressive Golden Ceremony. Seven new Human Touch Masters (1 from Croatia, 4 from Hungary and 2 from Spain) were inducted into the golden Human Touch Masters pin club, which now proudly numbers a total of 62 honorees. These are highly skilled customer-facing professionals who put our vision and idea of Human Touch into practice by living our Human Touch philosophy in their daily business, fulfilling each and every need of our customers and being the best of best in their professional field. We greatly value their efforts and congratulate them on their success.
ŠKODA WEB3 #1 Meetup
On 25 August, the VX WEB3 team, headed by Luca Zicconi, organised the first ŠKODA WEB3 Meetup, an informal offsite workshop for more than 50 people from a variety of strategic backgrounds. The event, held at the Endorfin conference facilities in Prague, included an opening speech by Martin Jahn, Board Member for Sales & Marketing, and Thorsten Nicklass, Head of Digital Development, Data and Rollout. One of the event’s main objectives was to introduce WEB3, Blockchain, Metaverse, NFTs and their potential for the carmaker, including an interesting panel talk discussion with WEB3 experts that was very well received by the audience.
